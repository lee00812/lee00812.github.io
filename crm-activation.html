CRM On-site Expereience Redesign

Role: Regional Assistant CRM Manager, Fossil Asia Pacific
Timing: First quarter 2022

The redesigned onsite sign-up experience is available in India and Japan with an updated layout and the ability to get the welcome coupon code directly on the site after signing up. As the first touchpoint for customers on the company website, each registration step requires thoughtful CRM strategy and design.

What? Improve the consumer enrolments into our CRM programs by website level interventions.

Why? 98% of visitors fail to sign-up on the company website, and the welcome email revenue has been dropping and we can dial up enrolments via the website.

How? CRM on-site experience redesign.

The Problem

~98% of visitors fail to sign-up on the company website, and the welcome email revenue has been dropping.

Strategic priorities: Grow CRM sign-up and drive the first purchase

KPIs: Improve CRM sign-up rate and conversion rate

Opportunities

Visual Design: Redesigned the look and feel of the sign-up window with brand color and imagery to reinforce the brand image.

Show Promo Code Instantly

Restructured the on-site sign-up experience.
Customers no longer have to check their inbox for the welcome coupon code through email as they did in the past. A promo code will be displayed instantly on the window after a customer signs up.

Use A Different Tone

Rephased the language.
Try to rephase the language within the brand tone
To encourage the CRM sign-up, some of the wordings like "Get 15% off" vs. "Decline Offer" have been used to test the result.

Prototype

In order to communicate the idea, a low-fidelity prototype was created using PowerPoint.

A/B Testing

Validate the ideals through A/B testing.
Version A: Pop up window
Version B: Full-width overlay

Results

KPI #1: Improve CRM Enrolments: A significant uplift has been seen in IN and JP. CRM sign-up rate increased over 220%
KPI #2: Conversion Rate: Conversion rate increased over 80%, while revenue increased over 50%
